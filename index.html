Anshu Ravi

Digital Media Artist

I am Anshu Ravi, a Palo Alto born and Bay Area raised digital media artist. I am currently working towards a Bachelor of Fine Arts at San Jose State University. I work with numerous technological mediums including programming code, 3D modeling, video, digital collage, and video games. The subject of my work follows an escape from existentialism through escapist daydreams into fantastical worlds beyond our own. I weave the tools of technology to bring these worlds to life. I have been featured in numerous galleries throughout San Jose State University along with the Fall 2023 Game Development Semester Showcase.
