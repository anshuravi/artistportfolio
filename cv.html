Profile

Anshu Ravi is a passionate game designer, visual effects artist, and interactive media student inspired to create immersive experiences. Anshu’s goal is to seamlessly blend creativity with technical finesse and create experiences to transport audiences into immersive worlds. She is currently exploring opportunities in game design, graphic design, vfx, web development, and interaction design.
